About

Angel Vasquez is a dedicated cybersecurity and IT professional with a strong passion for continuous learning. He holds an Associate degree in Cybersecurity and serves in the Air National Guard, specializing in Cyber Defense Operations. Angel is actively expanding his technical expertise through hands-on projects, including the development of a personal home lab.

He has experience in networking and system administration, including configuring VLANs, managing Active Directory in both Linux and Windows environments, and creating user accounts. His cybersecurity experience includes deploying and configuring SNORT IDS, building honeypots, and analyzing malware such as Zeus. Angel has also sharpened his offensive security skills through TryHackMe capture-the-flag challenges using Kali Linux.
